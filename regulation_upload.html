This page ingests the curriculum regulation PDF, extracts subjects, units, and topics, then loads the graph structure and relation edges into Neo4j so the notebook can use them immediately.

Subjects are read from the course list and linked to semesters.
Units and topics are parsed from the uploaded regulation pages.
Relations are created for prerequisites and topic structure.
Fallback summarization uses NLTK when a unit body needs a shorter topic readout.
